Challenges

Retail logistics has unique challenges

Unpredictable last-mile deliveries

Meeting tight delivery SLAs while managing thousands of daily orders across multiple fulfillment centers & carriers is a nightmare. Manual routing & static plans can’t keep up with fluctuating demand

Inefficient fleet utilization

Poorly optimized routes & low truckload utilization lead to escalating logistics costs. Market-based pricing fluctuations make cost control even harder.

Billing disputes & lack of real-time visibility

Manual reconciliation of COD payments, returns, and carrier invoices leads to disputes, delays & cash flow issues. Without real-time insights, identifying and resolving inefficiencies is slow and reactive
