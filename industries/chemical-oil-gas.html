Challenges

Chemical, Oil & Gas logistics has unique challenges

Compliance risks leading to penalties

Managing chemical logistics requires strict adherence to hazardous material transportation guidelines, including proper classification, labeling & documentation. Teams struggle with manual compliance checks and approvals for transit.

Lack of visibility for stakeholders

Internal teams & transporters lack real-time shipment visibility. Delayed approvals & unnoticed compliance breaches slow down operations & increase risks.

Untracked KPIs & cost deviations

Freight costs exceed budgets, but without clear KPI tracking, pinpointing inefficiencies is tough. Compliance trends, cost deviations & transporter performance remain unclear.

What is a TMS for Chemical Manufacturers, and why is it critical?

Transportation Management System (TMS) for chemical manufacturers ensures compliant, safe and efficient movement of hazardous and non-hazardous materials. Fretron’s TMS provides real-time control, automates dispatch approvals and helps maintain safety regulations, reducing delays, penalties and risks.

How does a TMS help ensure safety and regulatory compliance in chemical logistics?

Fretron’s TMS automates documentation for hazardous goods, manages approvals for sensitive shipments, and monitors route deviations to ensure compliance with safety norms and legal requirements.

How does a TMS for Chemicals support multi-modal transport and export documentation?

Fretron’s TMS supports road, rail and sea transport with integrated tracking and documentation workflows. It simplifies export logistics by automating checklists, container tracking and customs paperwork.

How can a TMS help reduce detention and improve dispatch efficiency in chemical logistics?

Fretron’s TMS optimizes gate-in, weighbridge and dispatch processes to minimize detention charges. It supports digital dock scheduling and real-time load assignment for faster turnaround.

How does a TMS improve visibility and control in high-risk chemical shipments?

With real-time GPS tracking, live alerts and exception-based monitoring, Fretron’s TMS ensures full visibility of chemical shipments. It also provides incident logs and analytics for continuous risk mitigation.
