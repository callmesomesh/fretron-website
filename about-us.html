Revolutionizing Logistics,
One Shipment at a Time!

Logistics is the backbone of every industry, yet it remains fragmented, inefficient, and costly. We’ve seen logistics teams struggle—juggling disconnected systems, manual processes & endless firefighting just to keep things moving. We believe there’s a better way.

As the name "Fretron" suggests, we are on a mission to take "freight online" & help businesses unlock the full potential of their supply chains. Our AI-driven, cloud-based Transportation Management System (TMS) transforms how companies manage logistics—making operations seamless, data-driven, and collaborative.

Our Mission-
Since 2017

To empower logistics teams with technology that simplifies, optimizes & connects every stakeholder—so businesses can scale faster, cut costs & deliver exceptional customer experiences.

Our story

Fretron wasn’t built in a boardroom—it was born out of the real-world struggles of logistics professionals dealing with broken processes, misaligned teams & outdated systems. For years, logistics teams have relied on manual tracking, disconnected software & MIS reports, leading to lost revenue, poor visibility, rising freight costs & constant firefighting. The lack of real-time data & automation made logistics operations reactive instead of strategic, frustrating teams, suppliers & customers alike.

In 2017, Puneet Agarwal & Sunil Dhaker set out to change this. Combining deep expertise in logistics, technology & automation, they envisioned a smarter way to manage freight—one that would eliminate inefficiencies, improve collaboration & drive intelligent decision-making. Fretron was built as a cloud-first, AI-powered Transportation Management System (TMS) designed to unify logistics stakeholders, digitize processes & provide real-time visibility & control.

Today, over 100+ enterprises, including industry leaders like VMart, CJ Darcl, Jindal, Exide & DHTC, trust Fretron to move millions of shipments efficiently. With AI-driven automation, predictive analytics & seamless integrations, we help businesses reduce costs, optimize operations & scale effortlessly. But this is just the beginning—our mission is to transform logistics, one shipment at a time.
