Fretron — 45-min ABM Deep Dive

Seed: Time: 45:00

Open-book. Design a realistic plan tied to business outcomes. We value clarity, guardrails, and ROI thinking.

Scenario

Fretron wants to run a 30-day ABM pilot for India-based manufacturers/retailers to book meetings for supply-chain leaders (and/or digital transformation). Budget ₹3L. You’ll pick 5 target accounts, outline an AI-assisted workflow, define guardrails, and project outcomes.

Task A — Micro-brief (10 pts)

In ≤4 bullets: target persona & pain, success KPI, constraints (data/compliance), key wedge.

Task B — Pick 5 accounts (25 pts)

Select 5 from the table. Avoid anomalies (non-ICP, DNC, competitor-locked).

Task C — Workflow & guardrails (20 pts)

Outline a 6-step agentic workflow with model/tool choices, two sentinel tests, fallback, and cost/latency caps.

Task D — ROI math (15 pts)

You’ll contact 1,500 prospects across chosen accounts. Baseline reply 1.5%, uplift 40% after better targeting. 30% of replies → meetings, 40% of meetings → SQLs. Expected meetings? SQLs?

Task E — Compliance & trust (10 pts)

Select all issues in: “We scraped emails from LinkedIn. As a GDPR-certified vendor we don’t need opt-outs. Click to confirm you want updates.”





Task F — Prototype prompt + eval plan (20 pts)

Write one prompt you’d actually ship for first-draft personalization and a 3-check evaluation plan to keep quality high.
